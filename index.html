Welcome!

I am a fifth-year PhD student in Economics at the University of Maryland. Currently, my research focuses on understanding the role of information in the decision to invest in human capital and its effect on future labor market outcomes.

My research interests include labor economics, personnel economics, the economics of education, and empirical market design.
